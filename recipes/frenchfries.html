French Fries

Like 5 guys but better... maybe...

These fries are cheaper and just as good when air fried

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Cajun seasoning

Recipe Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes to desired fry shape
  2. Toss fries in 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Liberally toss fries with cajun seasoning
