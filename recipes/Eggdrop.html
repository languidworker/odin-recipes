Egg Drop Recipe

An all-in-one meal for any time

This Korean born sandwhich serves as a hearty meal of mostly breakfast ingredients

Ingredients

  • Milk Bread - 2 slices
  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Sauce

Recipe Instructions

  1. Cook bacon to desired crispness
  2. Scramble egg in a pan with a pinch of salt and pepper
  3. Lightly toast bread
  4. Add sauce to both slices of bread
  5. Place egg on botton slice of bread, add bacon and top it off with 2nd slice of bread
